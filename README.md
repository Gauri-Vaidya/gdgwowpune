## About

Google Developer Group (GDG) Women of Pune is a community dedicated to empowering women in technology through knowledge sharing, mentorship, and networking opportunities. Our goal is to foster an inclusive environment where women developers can learn, grow, and collaborate on innovative projects.

## Mission

- Support women in tech through mentorship programs
- Organize workshops, hackathons, and technical talks
- Connect women developers with industry opportunities
- Promote diversity and inclusion in the tech ecosystem

## Events

We organize various events throughout the year including:
- Technical workshops
- Coding bootcamps
- Speaker sessions with industry experts
- Networking meetups
- Annual Women Techmakers conference
