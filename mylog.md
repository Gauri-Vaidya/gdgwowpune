# My GDG WOW Pune Experience

## Event: International Women's Day Celebration 2025
**Date:** March 8, 2025
**Location:** Tech Hub Coworking Space, Pune

## My Journey

When I first heard about the GDG Women of Pune event celebrating International Women's Day, I knew I had to attend. As someone passionate about technology and eager to connect with like-minded women in tech, this seemed like the perfect opportunity.

## The Morning Sessions

The day began with an inspiring keynote by Priya Sharma, a Senior Engineering Manager at Google, who spoke about her journey in tech and the importance of community support. Her story of overcoming imposter syndrome resonated deeply with many attendees, including myself.

This was followed by technical workshops that ran in parallel tracks:
- Machine Learning with TensorFlow
- Web Development with Flutter
- Cloud Architecture on Google Cloud Platform

I chose the Machine Learning workshop, where we built a simple image classification model. The hands-on approach made complex concepts accessible, and the mentors were incredibly patient and helpful.

## Networking Lunch

The networking lunch was perhaps one of the most valuable parts of the day. I connected with women from various backgrounds - students, professionals, entrepreneurs - all sharing a passion for technology. I exchanged contact information with several attendees and even found a potential mentor in the field I'm interested in.

## Afternoon Panel Discussion

The afternoon featured a panel discussion on "Breaking Barriers in Tech," with women leaders from various companies sharing their experiences and strategies for success. The candid conversations about challenges faced and overcome were both eye-opening and encouraging.

## Code Lab Session

The day concluded with a collaborative code lab where we worked in small groups to solve real-world problems using technology. My team worked on creating an accessibility tool for visually impaired users, and the experience of building something meaningful in just a few hours was incredibly rewarding.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Community matters**: Having a supportive community of women in tech can make a significant difference in one's career journey.
2. **Continuous learning**: The tech field is constantly evolving, and the willingness to learn is more important than knowing everything.
3. **Mentorship**: Both giving and receiving mentorship are crucial for professional growth.
4. **Diversity of thought**: Different perspectives lead to more innovative solutions.

## Next Steps

After attending this event, I'm motivated to:
- Contribute more actively to open-source projects
- Attend regular GDG meetups
- Consider speaking at a future event to share my own experiences
- Mentor junior women entering the tech field

I'm grateful to the GDG Women of Pune team for organizing such a thoughtful and impactful event. Looking forward to many more!
